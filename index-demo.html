STEVEN UNIVERSE CHARACTER DESIGN

This work showcases my skills in character design. I used the Autodesk Sketchbook app to achieve this project. The characters in this drawing are Steven and Amethyst from Steven Universe. It shows the characters front and back 3/4 view, the full frontal view, side view and back view. The idea of this character turn around is to give a 360 degree view of the characters.

BROTHERS

This is a character design of similar characters with different skin tones. Their outfits show a combination of streetwear and casual clothing. I carefully considered the colours to fit their individual skin tones and make them look more appealing. The groups composition gives the characters a strong feeling of solidarity and friendship. They’re 3 brothers from diverse backgrounds. When creating this drawing, I tried out different styles and techniques, facial expressions and body positions. I sketched out the outline of the drawings and the perfected it by adding their features like eyes, hair, clothing, sandals and some extras such as the beauty marks. Then I refined the drawing by giving each of them different skin tones and also adding colour to their outfit to bring the drawing to life.

FACELESS

This is a captivating piece that examines the influence of anonymity and focus on the sport itself. I named this work faceless because I wanted to emphasize the focus on the game rather than the player. In this artwork the ability and enthusiasm of the player are highlighted which captures the essence of basketball. I started this drawing by sketching out basic shapes and proportions of the basketball player. I then added more details like clothing, shoes and the basketball.

BART SIMPSON

This drawing captures a unique character from the Simpson’s with a combination of Post Malone and 21 Savages’ tattoos. To bring this drawing to life, I started by sketching out the Simpson character and then I studied the tattoos of Post Malone and 21 Savage to incorporate the key elements into the artwork. During the development stage, I refined the details, making sure the tattoos accurately represented those of Post Malone and 21 Savage and then incorporated a few more details like the piercings. The result is a visually striking piece of art that combines the iconic Simpson character with the tattoos of these artists.

EAGLE DRAWING

The drawing captures attention through its unique perspective, highlighting the eagle's curiosity with a sideways tilt. The artist experimented with various techniques to accurately depict the eagle's head, using graphite pencils and blending for the eye area. The process involved sketching the initial outline, refining proportions and details, and gradually adding intricate features such as feathers and the beak, drawing inspiration from different reference images.

STILL LIFE DRAWING

The drawing depicts an observational study of a kettle, utilizing the blending technique to achieve smooth color transitions for a realistic appearance. The artist began by experimenting with various shapes, sizes, and handle designs to find the most visually pleasing composition. Using the Autodesk SketchBook app, they sketched the kettle's outline with light pencil lines, refining it to add depth and detailing such as the spout and lid. Shading was applied to create a sense of volume and texture, enhancing the overall realism of the drawing.
